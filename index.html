About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an action-packed survival game where you face endless hordes of monsters. Choose from unique characters with special abilities and fight your way through increasingly difficult waves of enemies.

Collect experience points, level up, and unlock powerful upgrades to enhance your character's abilities. Discover rare items and combine them to create devastating combinations that will help you survive longer.

With its addictive gameplay, stunning visuals, and challenging progression system, Monster Survivors offers hours of entertainment for players of all skill levels.

Game Features

  • Multiple unique characters with different abilities
  • Endless waves of diverse monsters
  • RPG-style progression and upgrades
  • Collect and combine powerful items
  • Beautiful pixel art graphics
  • Dynamic difficulty scaling
  • Compete for high scores on the leaderboard

How to Play

1

Choose Your Character

Select from various unique characters, each with their own special abilities and starting stats.

2

Battle Monsters

Move around the map and automatically attack nearby monsters. Collect experience gems to level up.

3

Upgrade & Survive

Choose new abilities and upgrades when you level up. Survive as long as possible against increasingly difficult waves.

Game Controls

Keyboard Controls

  • WASD or Arrow Keys: Move your character
  • Space: Use special ability (when available)
  • ESC: Pause game
  • 1-4 Keys: Quick-select items

Mobile Controls

  • Virtual Joystick: Move your character
  • Action Button: Use special ability
  • Menu Button: Pause game
  • Item Icons: Tap to use items

Tips for Success

Beginner Tips

  • Focus on movement and avoiding monster clusters
  • Prioritize health and defensive upgrades early on
  • Collect all experience gems to level up faster
  • Don't stand still - keep moving to avoid damage
  • Learn each character's strengths and weaknesses

Advanced Strategies

  • Create synergistic item and ability combinations
  • Balance offensive and defensive upgrades
  • Use terrain and obstacles to funnel monsters
  • Save your special ability for emergency situations
  • Plan your upgrade path based on the monsters you're facing
